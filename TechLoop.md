# 🌐 TechLoop Official Wiki

Welcome to the internal knowledge base for **TechLoop**. This document outlines our identity, community-first approach, and the strategic pillars that guide our operations.

---

## 📖 1. Company Overview

TechLoop is an Australian online retailer specialising in **computers, gaming, and electronics**. We aim to redefine the purchasing experience by fostering a community-driven platform where tech enthusiasts are rewarded for their passion and shared knowledge.

- **The Big Idea:** "Technology for life".
- **The Big Goal:** To make every member feel part of a community that shares their passion for innovation.
- **Market Position:** We position ourselves as the only technology community in Australia that sells innovative consumer electronics in an era otherwise defined by poor customer service.

## 💎 3. Core Values

Our values dictate how we interact with our community and how we solve problems.

- **Cherish Community:** We believe we all belong to one community with a shared passion for technology. It is our responsibility to make this community special for everyone.
- **Helpful Humans:** we prioritise being the most helpful humans possible by creating amazing experiences through happiness, respect, and empathy.
- **Boycott Bullshit:** We put people before profits by providing open, honest, and transparent advice. We operate with total integrity. See our [[Tone of Voice]] guidelines for how this translates into copy.
- **Be Better:** We believe there is always a better way. We foster a culture of innovation, experimentation, and risk-taking to find creative solutions.

---

## 👥 4. Customer Segments

TechLoop serves a diverse range of users, each with unique needs and motivations.

1. **Tech Lovers:** Seek the latest innovative products from high-quality brands and startups. They want to feel rewarded for sharing their advice.
2. **Gamers:** Focused on the latest gaming innovations and community engagement.
3. **Digital Natives:** Demand instant gratification through fast delivery and convenient shopping. They make decisions based on peer opinions rather than brands.
4. **Mass Market:** Look for genuine advice from knowledgeable humans rather than pushy salespeople. They prioritise excellent customer service and respect.
5. **Tech Product Startups:** Sourcing partners who provide innovative products to our community.

---

## 🛠️ 5. The TechLoop Difference

We believe the traditional retail model is broken due to poor service, limited knowledge, and subpar after-sales support. We fix this through:

- **Crowdsourced Advice:** We combine the expert opinions of our team with independent, crowdsourced advice from our community so customers never second-guess a purchase.
- **Community-Driven Sourcing:** Our products are sourced specifically based on the wants and needs of our members.
- **Global Innovation:** We work directly with overseas brands to ensure Australians don't have to wait for the latest technology.
- **Superior Logistics:** Commitment to fast delivery, hassle-free returns, and dedicated community support.

---

## 📈 6. Business Operations

- **Revenue Streams:** Income is generated via product sales, merchandise, private label products, service fees, and commissions.
- **Key Channels:** We reach our community through our website, social media, community forums, blog/buying guides, and events.
- **Customer Relationships:** We focus on member loyalty through gamification, brand advocacy, and 24/7 customer service.
