# Monthly Retro

> Run on the last working day of each month to reflect and adjust. ~60-90 minutes.

## When to use

End of each calendar month. Schedule it as a recurring calendar block.

## Steps

1. **Review the month at a glance** — open the OKRs DB; assess Progress on each Q's objectives. Update Status (On Track / At Risk / Off Track).
2. **Wins** — list 3-5 meaningful wins from the month (shipped a feature, signed a supplier, hit a revenue milestone).
3. **Misses / regrets** — list 1-3 things that didn't go well or were dropped. Identify root cause briefly.
4. **Pattern check** — open the Decisions Log DB filtered to the last 30 days. Read your decisions back. Are they consistent? Any reversed?
5. **Customer feedback themes** — open Customer Feedback DB filtered to the last 30 days, sort by Frequency descending. Top 3 themes deserve attention next month.
6. **Adjust OKRs if needed** — if the world changed, edit the objectives. Don't sandbag; don't pretend.
7. **Set next month's focus** — pick 1 strategic priority + 1 operational priority. Create matching Projects if they don't exist.
8. **Brief**: write a 200-word summary of the month + the focus for next month. Save to General/Playbooks or as a Decisions Log entry.

## Notes / gotchas

- This is for *you*, not a board. Honest writing > polished writing.
- If 3 months in a row miss the same OKR, the OKR or the strategy is wrong — not the execution.
